## IT Risk Management Lab Portfolio

This repository contains a collection of hands-on cybersecurity labs completed as part of my IT Risk Management coursework at the University of South Florida. The labs demonstrate practical experience across risk assessment, vulnerability management, security controls, monitoring, incident response, and forensic analysis.
